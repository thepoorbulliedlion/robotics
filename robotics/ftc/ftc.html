FTC

FTC(First Robotics Competition) is another event similar to VEX, where teams are made and compete against each other. It encourages creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving as students design, build, and program robots to complete challenging tasks in themed game fields. Guided by adult mentors, participants develop skills in engineering, programming, and gracious professionalism. Beyond the competitions, FTC fosters a passion for STEM and empowers students to become future leaders and innovators.
